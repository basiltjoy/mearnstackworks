Welcome to Mount Carmel College

VISION

Provide a life, time, and trend sensitive education which is student-centered, focusing on the integral growth of the individual in order to form oneself to transform the society, one is part of.





To experience education with a touch of difference. Come on in to a world class institute of education built upon the tried and trusted educational tradition of the order of Carmelitues (O.Carm Fathers) based in Europe and the Americas that has a history of 800 years. Since its inception and affiliation in 2012 to the reputed Mahatma Gandhi University of Kerala, Mount Carmel College has made incredible progress in the history of education. Join us to experience the life of learning at our scenic campus in Karukadam on Kochi - Dhanushkhodi National Highway near Kothamangalam(5 Km) on the way to Muvattupuzha (7 Km).
